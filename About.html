About Hotel AR

AR is a brand known for boutique hotels, with each one having its own unique personality. The main AR website does a great job of highlighting the uniqueness of each property while staying true to the brand..

With a lot of very different properties under their brand umbrella, AR has found a great way to help guests find the right location for their needs. The “search by experience” section breaks properties\ up into mountain destinations, family destinations, spa destinations, urban destinations, and more. This shows a great focus on the customer and how they want to search for properties..
